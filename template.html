What is this?

This website provides RSS feeds for MyCrossword to notify solvers when their favourite setters publish new puzzles. This site is not affiliated with MyCrossword and is maintained for the community by setter Dyno.

What feeds are available here?

A feed for all new crosswords on MyCrossword, plus a feed for each individual setter. Feeds are updated every three hours and contain the 10 most recent puzzles from each setter. Just right-click "copy link" on the feed icon and paste into your feed reader. Feed URLs are currently case-sensitive.
